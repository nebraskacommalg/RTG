Fridays at 1:30 pm in Avery 351

In anticipation of Bhargav Bhatt's Rowlee lecture in March and Ray Heitmann's retirement conference in April, we are running a seminar on Big Cohen-Macaulay modules.

Current Schedule

Friday, January 31: Anna Brosowsky will speak on regular sequences, depth, and Cohen-Macaulay rings/modules

Friday, February 7: Jack Jeffries will speak on the Hochster-Roberts Theorem

Friday, February 14: Eloísa Grifo will speak on the homological conjectures and other consequences of CM modules

Friday, February 21: Tom Marley will speak on the direct summand conjecture in equal char and R^+ is CM in char p>0

Friday, February 28: Mark Walker will speak on the reduction to char p>0; big CM in char 0

Friday, March 7: Nawaj KC will speak on Perfectoid basics

Friday, March 14: Kesavan Mohana Sundaram will speak on the direct summand conjecture in mixed charactertic
